AI for Landcover Classification in Drylands


Model - Biome 8 - Temperate Grasslands, Savannas and Shrublands.

A CNN was trained on 20,381 Sentinel-2 samples (10 x 5 x 5 px) from the Temperate Grasslands, Savannas and Shrublands global biome. The neural network has an accuracy of 89% on the test dataset.
