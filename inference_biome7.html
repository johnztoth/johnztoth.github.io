AI for Landcover Classification in Drylands


Inference - Biome 7 - Tropical and Subtropical Grasslands, Savannas and Scrublands.

A convolutional neural network was trained on 89,297 Sentinel-2 samples (10 x 5 x 5 px) from the Tropical and Subtropical Grasslands, Savannas and Scrublands biome. The neural network has an accuracy of 86% on the test dataset.
