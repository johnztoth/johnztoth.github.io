AI for Landcover Classification in Drylands


Inference - Biome 7 - Tropical and Subtropical Grasslands, Savannas, and Shrublands.

A convolutional neural network was trained on 89,297 Sentinel-2 samples (10 x 5 x 5 px) from the Tropical and Subtropical Grasslands, Savannas, and Shrublands global biome. The neural network has an accuracy of 87% on the global test dataset. The figures below are from a 20 km x 20 km Sentinel-2 image for Chogoria, Kenya, acquired in 2024.
