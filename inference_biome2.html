AI for Landcover Classification in Drylands


Inference - Biome 2 - Tropical and Subtropical Dry Braodleaf Forests.

A convolutional neural network was trained on 28353 Sentinel-2 samples (10 x 5 x 5 px) from the Tropical and Subtropical Dry Braodleaf Forests biome. The neural network has an accuracy of 88% on the test dataset.
