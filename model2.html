AI for Landcover Classification in Drylands


Model - Biome 2 - Tropical and Subtropical Dry Broadleaf Forests.

A CNN was trained on 28,353 Sentinel-2 samples (10 x 5 x 5 px) from the Tropical and Subtropical Dry Broadleaf Forests global biome. The neural network has an accuracy of 88% on the test dataset.
