AI for Landcover Classification in Drylands


Model - Biome 7 - Tropical and Subtropical Grasslands, Savannas, and Shrublands.

A CNN was trained on 89,297 Sentinel-2 samples (10 x 5 x 5 px) from all Biome-7, Tropical and Subtropical Grasslands, Savannas, and Shrublands. The neural network has an accuracy of 86% on the test dataset.
