Masters by Research

John is a postgraduate research student at University of Leeds, UK, in the Faculty of Environment. His research project is to use AI for landcover classification in dryland environments. He has built a convolutional neural network to classify multispectral satellite imagery from the Sentinel-2 program at 10 m resolution. The neural network was trained on labeled landcover samples from global dryland biomes. The principal software libraries used were Pytorch, Geemap, and Geopandas, in a Jupyter Notebook environment.

The biomes of interest area (from Terrestrial Ecoregions of the World, Olson et al. 2001):

  • Tropical and Subtropical Dry Broadleaf Forests - Biome 2
  • Tropical and Subtropical Grasslands, Savannas, and Shrublands - Biome 7
  • Temparate Grasslands, Savannas, and Shrublands - Biome 8
  • Montane Grasslands and Shrublands - Biome 10
  • Mediterranean Forests, Woodlands, and Scrub - Biome 12
  • Deserts and Xeric Shrublands - Biome 13
