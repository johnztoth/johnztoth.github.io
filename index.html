John Toth

Masters by Research

John is a postgraduate research student at University of Leeds, UK. His research project is to use AI for landcover classification in dryland environments. He is building a convolutional neural network to classify multispectral satellite imagery from the Sentinel-2 program at 10 m resolution. The neural network is trained on labeled landcover samples from global dryland biomes. The main software libraries used are Pytorch, Geopandas, and Geemap.
